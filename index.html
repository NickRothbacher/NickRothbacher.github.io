Senior Research Data Engineer · Apple

GitHub LinkedIn

I'm a data scientist and research engineer with deep experience applying machine learning and data science to unique, multimodel applications, particularly in the context of public policy challenges. Most recently, I served as a Data Scientist at the Stanford RegLab (Regulation, Evaluation, and Governance Lab), where I developed computer vision systems and data pipelines to detect environmental violations and improve regulatory compliance under the Clean Water Act.

Starting March 2026, I'll be joining Apple as a Senior Research Data Engineer through TEKSystems, bringing my research engineering expertise to developing new products and features at scale.
